At the heart of every cup of coffee it is a combination of aesthetic allure and atmosphere changing taste that makes one stop and think about what has just happened.

You have the opportunity to taste something soo extraordinary that it renders all other thoughts pointless. Your mind now has one objective, to solve the puzzle it has been presented with.

Chain Coffee is a mobile coffee bar geared for events and functions. Our speciality is coffee beverages (as can be recognised in the name), however, we do a variety of beverages.

About Chain Coffee

Chain coffee was the dream of a young aspiring student.

The journey started when a coffee shop was willing to teach a student named Johannes Scribante how to make coffee. Knowing only on the basis of some say.... Although eager to learn, the shop could not pay Johannes for working there as no additional staff were needed. Knowing that learning a new skill is more valuable than the rands and cents that could be earned. He started working and learning. Three months later he was still working and learning.

Six months later Johannes drew up a business plan, took out a loan to purchase his machine and started Chain Coffee. With the support of the local coffee shop and many blessings Chain Coffee paid off the loan in two-and-a-half years, half-a-year earlier than the business plan.

Do not let circumstances prohibit your opportunity to learn. You only need the hunger to learn. You will find a way. Give it some time.

Our quotes and invoices work on a very simple principle.

Time and Guests.

We price for the amount of time we have to be available to make coffee. In conjunction with the time we ensure to bring at least the number of cups equal to 120% of the number of quests. This model allows the guests to drink coffee for free with the illusion of an unlimited supply of coffee.

This model has been tried an tested at over 80 weddings and has yet to fail.

Unless otherwise specified, set-up takes place one hour before the beverages are scheduled to be served.
